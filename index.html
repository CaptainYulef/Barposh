Barpösh

Barpösh is a artlang I'm developing, meant to be my ideal language. It would also come in useful for secret communication and logical writing.

Phonotactics and Grammer

The possible syllable structures are: V, CV, CVC.

Adjectives come after the word they're describing, though more grammatical things, such as markers and articles, are placed before.

If a word does not exist, it can be created by combining two word with a dash.
